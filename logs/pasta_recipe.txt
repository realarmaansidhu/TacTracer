Simple Garlic Pasta

Ingredients:
- 200 g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp grated Parmesan, optional

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Cook the spaghetti until al dente, then reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain.
3. Warm the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
4. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 30 to 45 seconds until fragrant.
5. Add the pasta to the pan and toss with a splash of reserved pasta water.
6. Season with salt and black pepper.
7. Finish with parsley and Parmesan if desired, then serve hot.
